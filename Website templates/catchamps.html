CAT Champs an interactive panel discussion

CAT Champs is an annual event organized by BITS Embryo. CAT Champs aims to aid CAT aspirants to receive guidance from our very own BITSian ‘CAT Champs’. In the words of a panelist of CAT Champs 6.0,” It is an honor to be on the panel of an event which I had attended last year for guidance.” Such exemplary panelists are our very own BITSians who have excelled the Common Aptitude Test with whopping percentiles of 99.5 and above. The entirety of the event is managed and organized by First Year students of BITS Embryo. It allows the freshers to learn the nitty-gritty of event hosting on such a large scale. The event witnesses over 500 footfalls in the telepresence room on the night of the panel discussion. Everything from contacting speakers to preparing mementos for the revered is supervised and executed by freshmen. CAT Champs is one of the few on-campus events that provide insight on a specific examination, catering to students of all years. Both freshmen and sophomores of BITS get to learn about CAT, while their seniors are guided from the get-go as they begin their preparation. Students are exposed to the field of management and also to the why’s and how’s of pursuing an MBA course- a bonus to having an engineering background. The panelists join the discussion by either being present on campus or through telepresence. The program kick starts with moderators introducing the panelists and there after posing them with some of the most frequently asked questions. The audience is exposed to various preparation strategies adopted by the panelists and gives them the chance to decide the most suitable one for themselves. Audience gets to know different solutions to the curriculum-specific issues that they faced and consequently tackled while preparing for CAT. The event is concluded with an interactive QnA session which allows members of the audience to have each of their queries addressed. Every year this event acts as a guiding force for aspiring students to carve their niche in one of the most well-known examinations and act as a torchbearer for their successors.
