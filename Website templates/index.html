About BITS Embryo

BITS Embryo is an institutional body established in 2008. It is a conjoint enterprise of students and faculty here at BITS to help increase the outreach of students in global research projects. Since its inception, through online talks and collaborative projects, it has aimed to provide BITS students with exposure and guidance in latest scientific and technology based cutting edge researches. Online talks augment the understanding of BITS courses and inspire students to pursue obscure areas of science and technology. BITS Embryo is striving to help students at BITS Pilani find their interest, build their knowledge and fill the panel with their accomplishments.

Since February, 2008 more than 200 lectures have been successfully conducted in areas as wide-ranging as Loop Quantum, Nanotechnology, Indian Space Program, Youth in Governance, Studying Abroad, Data Analytics, Mental Disorders, Self- Organisation.
